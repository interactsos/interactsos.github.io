Welcome to Interact Club of SOS

About Us

The Interact Club of SOS is dedicated to creating positive change in our community. We are a student-led organization focused on service, leadership development, and making meaningful contributions to society.

🎯 Focused

We concentrate on impactful projects that address real community needs.

🤝 Collaborative

We work together to achieve shared goals and support one another.

💪 Active

We consistently engage in meaningful service and community outreach.

Our Mission

To develop and promote friendly relations between young people of different nations, to encourage them to participate in community service activities with a focus on health, hunger, and humanitarian causes.

Core Values

  • Service: Making a real difference in our community
  • Leadership: Developing future leaders with integrity
  • Friendship: Building strong bonds across cultures
  • Development: Personal and professional growth

Recent Projects & Initiatives

August 5, 2025

Insight on Apiculture

Interact Club of SOS Hermann Gmeiner School conducted a program which included visit to bee farm and sharing of knowledge about different types of bee by professionals. We believe that this program benefited the students both theoretically and practically.

June 15, 2025

Plantation

Interact Club of SOS Hermann Gmeiner School Bharatpur organized a service oriented program where students of grade 1 and 2 planted seeds and plants all around the school. This program will light our environment and give satisfaction to all of us.

June 15, 2025

Earth Day Celebration

Happy earth day. On 15th June 2025, We Interact club of SOS Hermann Gmeiner School Bharatpur hosted a personal development program by conducting a drawing competition within grade one.

June 5, 2025

Environmental Action

World Environment Day, we Interact club of SOS Hermann Gmeiner School Bharatpur hosted a Slogan contest along with Science and Eco club. This self servicing program will help raise awareness in students and make our community a better place.

June 2, 2025

Keep the ground Clean

We, Interact club of SOS in collaboration with Science and Eco club and NJRC club conducted a school service program where we included the students from grade 4 to 10 to clean the school ground by plucking the grass.

March 1, 2025

Elderly Home Visit

We visited Rotary Karunalaya Elderly Home to spend a heartfelt day with the elderly, sharing smiles, stories, and laughter. Through “Samjhana Ko Chino”, we aimed to not just give gifts but also create beautiful memories with them. Their warmth and blessings made this experience truly special.

February 2, 2025

Spelling Contest

Interact Club of SOS HGS successfully organized a Spelling Contest for students of Grades 4, 5, and 6. This initiative aimed to foster linguistic proficiency, enhance extracurricular engagement, and encourage healthy academic competition among students.

September 1, 2024

Waste Management

Interact Club of SOS hgs organized an orientation program on waste management for Grades 7 and 8. The session aimed to educate students on the importance of responsible waste disposal and the methods to manage waste effectively. The program included presentations on waste types, their environmental impact, and practical tips on reducing, reusing, and recycling.

August 18, 2024

Raksha Bandhan

Interact club of SOS Hermann Gmeiner School Bharatpur hosted a coloring and drawing competition for students in grades 1 to 4 in celebration of the auspicious occasion of Raksha Bandhan. The theme for the project was “Bandan” emphasizing the bonds of love and protection of brother and sister to the festival.
